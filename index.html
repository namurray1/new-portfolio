My name is Alex Murray. I was born in Manhattan, NY. I moved to Charlotte as a child where I also attended secondary school and College. I graduated from the University of North Carolina at Charlotte with a degree in Political Science where I also double majored in Philosophy (with a minor in Women's Studies). What did I do next, you ask? Well, I did what any self-respecting liberal arts major would do. I went into the hospitality business, where I bartended and managed night clubs and saloons, followed by a 12 year career in the banking industry. Along the way, I maintained a childhood interest in technology which ultimately lead me into Web Development.

Now, I enjoy coding. I really enjoy coding. There are so many things that you can do. I mean... you can change the world! Web Development is like an artist with a paint brush and canvas or a poet with a pen and a piece of parchment. I immensely enjoy the process. And, web development is not easy. It requires a meticulous eye, patience, the love of constant reading, and the pursuit of knowledge. Along the way, I have also found a way to give back to the community by doing pro bono work with Code for Charlotte, a coding brigade. Civic hacking is a great way to bring technology and, specifically the internet, to the people.

Now, I don' want you to think that I am all thumb drives, keyboards, and mice. There is life outside of coding! I am an avid gardener, music lover, very amateur photographer, supporter of the arts, and animal advocate. I also enjoy traveling the world...
