My name is Alex Murray. I was born in Manhattan, NY. I moved to North Carolina as a child where I also attended secondary school and College. I graduated from the University of North Carolina at Charlotte with a degree in Political Science where I also double majored in Philosophy (with a minor in Women's Studies). What did I do next, you ask? Well, I did what any self-respecting liberal arts major would do. I went into the hospitality business, where I bartended and managed night clubs and saloons, followed by a 12 year career in the banking industry. Along the way, I maintained a childhood interest in technology which ultimately lead me into Web Development.

Now, I enjoy coding. I really enjoy coding. There are so many things that you can do. I mean... you can change the world with coding! Web development is like an artist with a paint brush and canvas OR a poet with a pen and a piece of parchment. I immensely enjoy the process as you see things develop and come to life. Web development is not easy. It requires a meticulous eye, patience, voracious reading, and the constant pursuit of knowledge. Along the way, I have also found a way to give back to the community by doing pro bono work with Code for Charlotte. Civic hacking is a great way to bring technology and government to the people.

I don't want you to think that I'm all thumb drives, keyboards, and mice. There is life outside of coding! I am an avid gardener, music lover, a very amateur photographer, a supporter of the arts, and animal advocate. I also enjoy traveling the with my girlfriend and our dog, Murphy. In the meantime, I will continue to cultivate the karass...
